

# ICCR Global — Premium Institutional Website Redesign

A world-class, single-page institutional website for the Institute of Civilisation, Culture & Religion, designed to impress international diplomats, scholars, and policymakers.

---

## 1. Design System & Visual Identity

- **Color palette** inspired by Indian heritage: deep sandstone (#8B6914), copper (#B87333), deep navy (#1B2A4A), ivory (#FAFAF5), warm stone (#F5F0E8), with modern neutrals
- **Typography**: Clean, authoritative serif for headings, modern sans-serif for body — strong hierarchy with generous spacing
- **Design language**: Refined, institutional, minimal — no flashy elements, high contrast for readability

## 2. Sticky Header with Shrink Effect

- Gradient header (sandstone to copper tones) with ICCR logo and institute name
- Desktop: full horizontal navigation menu with smooth hover underlines
- Mobile: elegant hamburger menu with slide-out navigation panel
- Header shrinks on scroll for a polished, premium feel

## 3. Hero Section — Maximum Impact

- Full-viewport image slider with smooth crossfade transitions (using placeholder heritage images)
- Glassmorphism overlay card with institute name, tagline, and "Ek Bharat, Shreshth Bharat" motto
- Subtle Ken Burns (slow zoom/pan) effect on images for cinematic feel
- A "Learn More" CTA button that scrolls down smoothly

## 4. Content Sections — Elegant Cards & Visual Storytelling

All sections use the same content but with dramatically improved layout:

- **Background & Vision**: Split layout with a decorative accent border, motto highlighted in a styled badge, paragraph text with premium spacing
- **Who We Are**: Card-based layout with an icon or decorative element, clean readable typography
- **What We Do / Programs & Exposure**: Grid of feature cards with subtle icons representing each focus area
- **Events & Dialogue (Global Conclave)**: Highlighted section with a distinct background tone, key details in an organized card layout
- **Collaboration & Partnership**: Clean CTA section with email prominently displayed and a styled "Get in Touch" button

## 5. Scroll & Reveal Animations

- Sections fade in and slide up as they enter the viewport
- Subtle staggered animations for card grids
- Smooth, elegant — no flashy or distracting effects

## 6. Premium Footer

- Matching gradient background (sandstone to copper)
- Two-column grid: About blurb + Contact info
- Links to all sections for quick navigation
- Institutional branding and copyright

## 7. Responsive Design

- Desktop: spacious layouts, full navigation bar, side-by-side content
- Tablet: adapted grid layouts, maintained readability
- Mobile: stacked layouts, hamburger menu, touch-friendly spacing

## 8. Micro-Interactions & Polish

- Hover effects on navigation links (underline animations)
- Button hover states with smooth transitions
- Card hover lift effects with subtle shadows
- Smooth scroll behavior throughout the page
